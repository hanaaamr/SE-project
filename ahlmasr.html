Ahl Masr

About Us

The Ahl Masr Trauma and Burn Hospital is an empowerment agency for burn and trauma victims
Established in Cairo in 2013 under registration number 728/2013, Ahl Masr Foundation is the first non-profit organization of its kind in the Middle-East and Africa dedicated to treating, preventing, and researching trauma and burn injuries.
